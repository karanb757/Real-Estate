Madhya Pradesh Style Bedroom

Madhya Pradesh is not only the heart of the country but is also the nucleus of the cultures of India. It is the hub of various cultures that meet in the country’s middle, making it a rendezvous of Indian cultures, religions, communities, and literature. The infrastructural designs of MP reflect the elements of cultural beauty and ethnicity. Although times have made the traditions step into the modern world, the inhabitants of MP prefer to stay tied to their ancestors through art and language. If you’ve ever visited MP, you would have seen the unique artforms that give you a tour of their ancestors. The Gond Paintings of Madhya Pradesh draw immense attention and are still installed in the homes of many.

MP Style Bedroom with Traditional Art Blended with Modern Architectural Designs

If you want a home with a traditional touch in MP, paintings or artwork with bamboo and cane coverings, jute works, durries, folk paintings, iron crafts, carpet weaving are excellent decor ideas. Metal crafts and ornaments, modernized lamps and shelves, and Paper Mache and stone carvings give you an ethnic feel to the homely appearance of your bedroom in the cities of Madhya Pradesh.
